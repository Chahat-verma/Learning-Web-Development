Html entities are used to represent special characters in HTML.

Here are some common HTML entities:

  • & - Ampersand
  • < - Less than
  • > - Greater than
  • " - Double quote
  • ' - Single quote
  •   - Non-breaking space
  • © - Copyright
  • ® - Registered trademark
  • € - Euro sign
  • £ - Pound sign
  • ¥ - Yen sign
  • ¢ - Cent sign
  • § - Section sign
  • ° - Degree sign
  • × - Multiplication sign
  • ÷ - Division sign
  • … - Horizontal ellipsis
  • — - Em dash
  • – - En dash
  • « - Left-pointing double angle quotation mark
  • » - Right-pointing double angle quotation mark
  • ‘ - Left single quotation mark
  • ’ - Right single quotation mark
  • “ - Left double quotation mark
  • ” - Right double quotation mark
  • • - Bullet
  • ′ - Prime (foot) symbol
  • ″ - Double prime (inch) symbol
  • ¹ - Superscript one
  • ² - Superscript two
  • ³ - Superscript three
  • ¼ - Fraction one quarter
  • ½ - Fraction one half
